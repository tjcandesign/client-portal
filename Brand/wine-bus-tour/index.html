The Day

A full-day story arc. Every great trip has acts.

SCENE 01THE DEPARTURE
🚌
9:00 AM
Board the Bus
Pickup at Melissa’s — 1002 Girard St NW. Everyone loads up, cooler goes in the back, and someone cracks the first bottle of bubbly. The bus rolls out of DC heading west toward the Blue Ridge.
📍 1002 Girard St NW, DC
SCENE 02THE RIDE OUT
🛣
9:00 — 10:00 AM
One Hour Through Virginia
The highway gives way to rolling hills and horse fences. This is your pre-game window — wine on the bus, music up, main character energy activated.
SCENE 03FIRST POUR
🍷
10:15 AM — 12:00 PM
Three Fox Vineyards
50 acres of meadow along Crooked Run Creek. Italian-style wines, a craft brewery for non-wine moments, and the kind of golden morning light that was made for group photos. Wine flights, explore the property, settle in.
🍇 Italian varietals + craft brewery
🧺
12:00 — 12:30 PM
Picnic in the Meadow
Spread the blanket along the creek. Trader Joe’s charcuterie, brie, prosciutto, grapes, dark chocolate — the whole spread. This is the midday reset before round two.
SCENE 05ROLLING
🚌
12:45 PM
Roll to Winery #2
Quick 20-minute cruise through Loudoun County. Regroup on the bus, compare tasting notes, and argue about who has the best palate.
SCENE 06THE VINEYARD
🍇
1:15 — 3:00 PM
Breaux Vineyards
404 acres of rolling countryside and some of Virginia’s best wines. Tastings, vineyard views, food trucks usually on weekends. The afternoon golden hour starts here.
🏔 404 acres · vineyard views
SCENE 07THE WILDCARD
✨
3:30 — 5:00 PM
Optional Third Stop
Cana Vineyards or Greenhill — only if the group is feeling it. If the vibe says “head home,” we head home. No pressure, no FOMO, just vibes.
⚡ FLEX — Skip if you want
🌅
5:00 PM
Golden Hour Home
Board the bus one last time. Windows down, music on, the sun going gold over the Virginia hills. Everyone recaps their favorite pour, their favorite moment. This is the closing scene.
SCENE 09FIN
🏠
6:15 — 6:30 PM
Back in DC
Dropoff at Melissa’s. A full day, done right. Now someone order dinner.
Flex: The third winery is totally optional. Two stops plus a picnic = a perfectly relaxed day without rushing.

Picnic Plan

Trader Joe's run + cooler on the bus. Eat at Three Fox's meadow.

Cheese & Charcuterie

  • Assorted cheese (brie, gouda, manchego)
  • Prosciutto, salami, sopressata
  • Crackers and baguette slices

Snacks & Sides

  • Hummus and pita chips
  • Mixed nuts and dried fruit
  • Fresh fruit (grapes, strawberries, apple slices)
  • Dark chocolate

Drinks

  • Water bottles (plenty!)
  • Sparkling water
  • Wine for the bus ride

Supplies

  • Large cooler with ice
  • Napkins, plates, utensils
  • Cutting board and knife
  • Trash bags
